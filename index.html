About Us

kRtyaVed is a platform dedicated to the world of performing arts, where 'kRtya' signifies 'Performance' and 'Ved' embodies 'Divine knowledge'.

Here, you can fully immerse yourself in a rich tapestry of dance, yoga, music, martial arts, and fitness.
Our holistic approach nurtures artists, encompassing internal well-being, musical knowledge, flexibility through fitness, and even the ancient martial art of Kalaripayattu. Additionally, kRtyaVed proudly offers IYO Yoga teacher's training courses. kRtyaVed serves as a vibrant hub for artists to convene, fostering innovative thinking and the fusion of diverse art forms, inspiring the creation of new trends.

OUR VISION

To support and nurture young, budding and enthusiastic performers and impart quality education in traditional and contemporary forms of dance, music, arts and fitness

OUR MISSION

The academy's mission is to nurture artistic growth, fostering the transformation of individuals into accomplished artists. We strive to cultivate a vibrant community of artists, performers, and mentors specializing in Indian classical and contemporary art forms. Our dedicated team of professional instructors is committed to inspiring, training, and nurturing students of all ages and backgrounds
