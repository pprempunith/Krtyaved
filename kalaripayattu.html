  1. Course Kalaripayattu

The Warrior Flows Through You

We offer authentic Kalaripayattu classes - the ancient Indian martial art form that combines self-defense, physical fitness, and artistic expression. Kalaripayattu has a rich history dating back over 3,000 years, making it one of the world's oldest martial arts. Kalaripayattu is more than just a martial art; it's a holistic discipline that encompasses:

Self-Defense:
Learn effective techniques for self-defense and build confidence in your ability to protect yourself.

Physical Fitness:
Kalaripayattu promotes strength, flexibility, endurance, and balance through a unique blend of movements.

Mental Discipline:
Sharpen your focus, concentration, and discipline as you progress in your training.

Cultural Heritage:
Immerse yourself in the cultural traditions of Kerala, where Kalaripayattu originated..
